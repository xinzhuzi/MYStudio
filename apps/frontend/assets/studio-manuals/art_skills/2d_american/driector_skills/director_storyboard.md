# 分镜提示词 · 2D美式动画

## 固定风格锚定词

2D美式动画，western 2D cartoon animation，bold outline, exaggerated expression, energetic pose, graphic background，bright studio color, clean flat light, playful contrast，solid color fills, thick contour, simplified forms

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | bright studio color, clean flat light, playful contrast 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (western 2D cartoon animation:1.3), (bold outline, exaggerated expression, energetic pose, graphic background:1.18), (bright studio color, clean flat light, playful contrast:1.1), solid color fills, thick contour, simplified forms, clean composition, readable silhouette, high detail, finished illustration

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality:1.4), blurry, messy lineart, bad anatomy, 3D render, photorealistic, CGI, watermark, signature, text, delicate anime line, realistic shading, 3D render, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- delicate anime line, realistic shading, 3D render。
- 让镜头语言破坏“2D 动画 / 插画”的媒介边界。
