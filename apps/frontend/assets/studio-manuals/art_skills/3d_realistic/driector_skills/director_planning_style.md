# 导演规划 · 3D写实

## 一、色调体系

- **色彩基线**：neutral film grade, slate grey, warm highlight。
- **情绪绑定**：温柔段落降低对比，冲突段落强化明暗，高潮段落提高主体边缘光。
- **禁用色域**：cartoon, anime, flat illustration, low poly, plastic skin。

## 二、光影方案

- **主光方案**：ray-traced lighting, cinematic depth of field, controlled contrast。
- **层次原则**：主体、背景和道具要有明确层次，不允许画面糊成一片。
- **媒介边界**：全片保持 3D 渲染，不混入错媒介词。

## 三、质感方向

- **质感锚点**：micro surface detail, natural imperfections, realistic material response。
- **提示词基线**：(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (photorealistic 3D cinematic render:1.3), (highly detailed texture, realistic skin shader, complex fabric, accurate scale:1.18), (ray-traced lighting, cinematic depth of field, controlled contrast:1.1), micro surface detail, natural imperfections, realistic material response, sharp focus, detailed background, polished composition。
- **反向规避**：(worst quality, low quality, bad quality:1.4), blurry, fuzzy, distorted, out of focus, malformed body, extra limbs, watermark, signature, text, cartoon, anime, flat illustration, low poly, plastic skin。

## 四、段落规划

| 段落类型 | 视觉策略 |
|---|---|
| 开场 | 用代表性场景建立 3D写实 的风格基线 |
| 人物登场 | 强化主体轮廓、服饰/材质和面部识别点 |
| 冲突段落 | 增强构图张力和光影对比，但不破坏身份连续 |
| 收束段落 | 减少复杂元素，回到清晰、稳定、可延续的视觉基线 |
