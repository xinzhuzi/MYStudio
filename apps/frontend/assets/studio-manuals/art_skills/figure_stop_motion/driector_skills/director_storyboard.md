# 分镜提示词 · 手办定格动画

## 固定风格锚定词

手办定格动画，action figure stop-motion photography，toy figure body, articulated pose, miniature prop, display diorama，macro studio light, rim light on plastic surface, shallow depth，PVC material, molded seams, toy-scale detail

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | macro studio light, rim light on plastic surface, shallow depth 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (action figure stop-motion photography:1.3), (toy figure body, articulated pose, miniature prop, display diorama:1.18), (macro studio light, rim light on plastic surface, shallow depth:1.1), PVC material, molded seams, toy-scale detail, macro photography, tactile detail, frame-by-frame charm, high detail

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality:1.4), fluid CGI animation, 2D anime, photorealistic human scale, smooth digital texture, bad anatomy, watermark, signature, text, human skin realism, 2D drawing, life-size body, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- human skin realism, 2D drawing, life-size body。
- 让镜头语言破坏“定格动画”的媒介边界。
