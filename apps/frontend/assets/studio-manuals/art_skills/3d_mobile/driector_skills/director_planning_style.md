# 导演规划 · 3D手游

## 一、色调体系

- **色彩基线**：blue sky, fresh green, heroic gold。
- **情绪绑定**：温柔段落降低对比，冲突段落强化明暗，高潮段落提高主体边缘光。
- **禁用色域**：photorealistic noise, rough sketch, pixelated low quality。

## 二、光影方案

- **主光方案**：bright outdoor light, soft ambient light, polished game look。
- **层次原则**：主体、背景和道具要有明确层次，不允许画面糊成一片。
- **媒介边界**：全片保持 3D 渲染，不混入错媒介词。

## 三、质感方向

- **质感锚点**：optimized clean material, stylized cloth and metal, vivid but controlled color。
- **提示词基线**：(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (stylized mobile game 3D render:1.3), (hero character design, clean fantasy outfit, readable game asset silhouette:1.18), (bright outdoor light, soft ambient light, polished game look:1.1), optimized clean material, stylized cloth and metal, vivid but controlled color, sharp focus, detailed background, polished composition。
- **反向规避**：(worst quality, low quality, bad quality:1.4), blurry, fuzzy, distorted, out of focus, malformed body, extra limbs, watermark, signature, text, photorealistic noise, rough sketch, pixelated low quality。

## 四、段落规划

| 段落类型 | 视觉策略 |
|---|---|
| 开场 | 用代表性场景建立 3D手游 的风格基线 |
| 人物登场 | 强化主体轮廓、服饰/材质和面部识别点 |
| 冲突段落 | 增强构图张力和光影对比，但不破坏身份连续 |
| 收束段落 | 减少复杂元素，回到清晰、稳定、可延续的视觉基线 |
