# 分镜提示词 · 3D渲染2D

## 固定风格锚定词

3D渲染2D，anime-inspired cel shaded 3D render，toon linework, cel shaded body, vibrant fantasy setting, clean anime face，bright rim light, soft global illumination, colorful sky light，toon material, sharp edge highlights, controlled flat shadow

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | bright rim light, soft global illumination, colorful sky light 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (anime-inspired cel shaded 3D render:1.3), (toon linework, cel shaded body, vibrant fantasy setting, clean anime face:1.18), (bright rim light, soft global illumination, colorful sky light:1.1), toon material, sharp edge highlights, controlled flat shadow, sharp focus, detailed background, polished composition

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality, bad quality:1.4), blurry, fuzzy, distorted, out of focus, malformed body, extra limbs, watermark, signature, text, photorealistic skin, heavy realism, rough sketch, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- photorealistic skin, heavy realism, rough sketch。
- 让镜头语言破坏“3D 渲染”的媒介边界。
