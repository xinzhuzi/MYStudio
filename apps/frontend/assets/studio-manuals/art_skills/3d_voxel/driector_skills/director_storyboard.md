# 分镜提示词 · 3D方块世界

## 固定风格锚定词

3D方块世界，voxel block world 3D art，cubic character, blocky trees, grid-based village, isometric readability，bright daylight, crisp shadow, cheerful atmosphere，voxel cubes, pixel-like material, clean toy blocks

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | bright daylight, crisp shadow, cheerful atmosphere 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (voxel block world 3D art:1.3), (cubic character, blocky trees, grid-based village, isometric readability:1.18), (bright daylight, crisp shadow, cheerful atmosphere:1.1), voxel cubes, pixel-like material, clean toy blocks, sharp focus, detailed background, polished composition

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality, bad quality:1.4), blurry, fuzzy, distorted, out of focus, malformed body, extra limbs, watermark, signature, text, round organic forms, smooth realistic texture, blur, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- round organic forms, smooth realistic texture, blur。
- 让镜头语言破坏“3D 渲染”的媒介边界。
