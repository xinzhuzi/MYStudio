# 分镜提示词 · 真人复古武侠

## 固定风格锚定词

真人复古武侠，vintage live-action wuxia cinema，martial arts stance, old inn, forest duel, practical costume，hard side light, dusty backlight, retro film contrast，film grain, worn fabric, real weapon surface

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | hard side light, dusty backlight, retro film contrast 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (vintage live-action wuxia cinema:1.3), (martial arts stance, old inn, forest duel, practical costume:1.18), (hard side light, dusty backlight, retro film contrast:1.1), film grain, worn fabric, real weapon surface, real lens optics, natural skin texture, cinematic framing, high detail

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality:1.4), 3D render, CGI, anime, illustration, cartoon, plastic skin, over-smoothed face, bad anatomy, watermark, signature, text, modern clothing, sci-fi tech, CGI magic excess, anime, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- modern clothing, sci-fi tech, CGI magic excess, anime。
- 让镜头语言破坏“真人摄影”的媒介边界。
