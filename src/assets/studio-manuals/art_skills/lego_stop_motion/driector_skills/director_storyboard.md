# 分镜提示词 · 积木定格动画

## 固定风格锚定词

积木定格动画，brick toy stop-motion animation，block figure, construction bricks, modular toy town, snapped-together props，macro toy light, crisp shadow, colorful plastic reflection，hard plastic brick surface, molded studs, clean toy edges

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | macro toy light, crisp shadow, colorful plastic reflection 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (brick toy stop-motion animation:1.3), (block figure, construction bricks, modular toy town, snapped-together props:1.18), (macro toy light, crisp shadow, colorful plastic reflection:1.1), hard plastic brick surface, molded studs, clean toy edges, macro photography, tactile detail, frame-by-frame charm, high detail

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality:1.4), fluid CGI animation, 2D anime, photorealistic human scale, smooth digital texture, bad anatomy, watermark, signature, text, soft clay, melted curved shapes, realistic human body, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- soft clay, melted curved shapes, realistic human body。
- 让镜头语言破坏“定格动画”的媒介边界。
