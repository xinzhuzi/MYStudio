# 分镜提示词 · 2D经典圆线漫画

## 固定风格锚定词

2D经典圆线漫画，classic round-line retro manga，large expressive eyes, rounded face, simple futuristic prop, iconic silhouette，clean flat light, vintage print tone, bright clarity，smooth black line, simple shading, old comic texture

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | clean flat light, vintage print tone, bright clarity 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (classic round-line retro manga:1.3), (large expressive eyes, rounded face, simple futuristic prop, iconic silhouette:1.18), (clean flat light, vintage print tone, bright clarity:1.1), smooth black line, simple shading, old comic texture, clean composition, readable silhouette, high detail, finished illustration

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality:1.4), blurry, messy lineart, bad anatomy, 3D render, photorealistic, CGI, watermark, signature, text, modern hyper-detailed anime, sharp realistic rendering, 3D, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- modern hyper-detailed anime, sharp realistic rendering, 3D。
- 让镜头语言破坏“2D 动画 / 插画”的媒介边界。
