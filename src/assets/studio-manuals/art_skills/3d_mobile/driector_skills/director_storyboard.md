# 分镜提示词 · 3D手游

## 固定风格锚定词

3D手游，stylized mobile game 3D render，hero character design, clean fantasy outfit, readable game asset silhouette，bright outdoor light, soft ambient light, polished game look，optimized clean material, stylized cloth and metal, vivid but controlled color

## 情绪到画面词映射

| 情绪 | 面容/主体 | 光影 | 镜头建议 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 温柔 | 目光柔和、动作克制 | bright outdoor light, soft ambient light, polished game look 的柔化版本 | 中近景，缓慢推近 |
| 紧张 | 表情收紧、主体轮廓明确 | 对比增强、暗部加深 | 低角度或侧逆光 |
| 释然 | 姿态放松、背景打开 | 主光回暖、空气感增强 | 远景到中景过渡 |
| 爆发 | 动作明确、重心前压 | 强轮廓光、动势线强化 | 动态构图但不破坏识别 |

## 画质锁定词

(best quality, masterpiece, high detailed:1.2), (stylized mobile game 3D render:1.3), (hero character design, clean fantasy outfit, readable game asset silhouette:1.18), (bright outdoor light, soft ambient light, polished game look:1.1), optimized clean material, stylized cloth and metal, vivid but controlled color, sharp focus, detailed background, polished composition

## 负向词模板

(worst quality, low quality, bad quality:1.4), blurry, fuzzy, distorted, out of focus, malformed body, extra limbs, watermark, signature, text, photorealistic noise, rough sketch, pixelated low quality, subtitles, captions, watermark, title overlay, UI text

## 禁止项

- 直接照搬具体作品、作者、厂牌或版权角色。
- photorealistic noise, rough sketch, pixelated low quality。
- 让镜头语言破坏“3D 渲染”的媒介边界。
